About

With the ever expanding amount of information being posted online each and every day, it can often be a challenge to find any real nuggets of wisdom amongst the piles of babble. My goal with this page is to separate the music from the noise, and collect a few hidden gems here in an immortal tribute to the creativity and ingenuity of human thought.

This project originally began as a YouTube playlist, but that eventually felt too limiting, so I finally decided to create a dedicated place for it here.
Forever a work in progress...

- Pedro Contipelli
